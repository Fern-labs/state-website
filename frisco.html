Frisco

Affectionately called “Main Street of the Rockies” for it's charming downtown, Frisco clocks in at just under 2 square miles. Frisco is the perfect springboard to all sorts of outdoor adventures that extend well beyond our town boundaries, including skiing, snowshoeing, hiking, biking, sailing, and camping.

Frisco is wonderful to vist in the winter.

City Information

  • Population is 2,804
  • The town of Frisco, Colorado was founded in 1873 by Henry Recen.
  • Frisco, Colorado is located in Summit County, Colorado
  • Frisco, Colorado, is generally considered a rural or small-town community.
  • Median household income in Frisco is approximately $101,140. Median household income for Colorado overall is around $78,127.38.
