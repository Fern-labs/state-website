Captial City Denver

Denver, the capital of Colorado, is an American metropolis dating to the Old West era. Larimer Square, the city’s oldest block, features landmark 19th-century buildings. Museums include the Denver Art Museum, an ultramodern complex known for its collection of indigenous works, and the mansion of famed Titanic survivor Molly Brown

Denver, also home to the Denver Broncos.

City Information

  1. Denver's population is approximately 716,000.
  2. Denver was incorporated in 1861.
  3. Denver is located in the Front Range Urban Corridor in north-central Colorado, nestled against the eastern edge of the Rocky Mountains.
  4. Denver is classified as an urban city. It serves as both a city and a county and is the capital of Colorado.
  5. Denver’s median household income is around $79,000. This is higher than the Colorado state median, which is around $77,000.
