Colorado

Colorado, a western U.S. state, has a diverse landscape of arid desert, river canyons and snow-covered Rocky Mountains, which are partly protected by Rocky Mountain National Park.

Colorado is noted for its landscape of mountains, forests, high plains, mesas, canyons, plateaus, rivers, and desert lands.

State Information

  1. Population: 5.84 million
  2. Statehood granted: August 1, 1876
  3. Colorado is in the Mountain West subregion of the Western United States.
  4. The average Income in Colorado is $78,127.38 per year
